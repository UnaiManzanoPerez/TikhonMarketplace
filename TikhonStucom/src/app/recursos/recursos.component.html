About

We are three web development students that made a webpage for our final project, we have created a trading marketplace site that allows people to buy and sell online products from video games, such as game keys, game items or games itselves in a simple way. We got this idea from the concept of transforming other webpages and applications like Wallapop or Facebook Marketplaces but for online goods.

Services

We offer a trading service that allows our client to sell and buy all kinds of digital products. Giving our customers and clients a simple interface to trade their goods.
Currently, we have three payment methods ( Paypal, Credit/Debit card, and Bitcoin) to either pay as a buyer or get payed as a seller.
Every seller has his public profile where buyers can make reviews about the products they acquired and rate them, so buyers can know who are they purchasing from.

Clients

Our clients are the sellers! The requirements to be able to sell are pretty basic, just some basic information like ID, Name and address and you are ready to sell after adding the payment information on the wallet tab.
