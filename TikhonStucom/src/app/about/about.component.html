About us

We are three web development students that made a webpage for our final project, we have created a trading marketplace site that allows people to buy and sell online products from video games, such as game keys, game items or games itselves in a simple way. We got this idea from the concept of transforming other webpages and applications like Wallapop or Facebook Marketplaces but for online goods.
